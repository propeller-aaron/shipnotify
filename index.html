You can use this page as a direct carrier redirect by including a tracking number in the URL. This is perfect for email links and automated notifications.

URL Format:

https://shipnotify.top?tn=TRACKING_NUMBER

Examples:

  • UPS: https://shipnotify.top?tn=1Z999AA10123456784
  • USPS: https://shipnotify.top?tn=9400111899223197428497
  • FedEx: https://shipnotify.top?tn=123456789012
  • DHL: https://shipnotify.top?tn=1234567890

How it works: When a tracking number is provided in the URL, the page automatically detects the carrier (FedEx, USPS, UPS, or DHL) and redirects to USPS, UPS, DHL or FedEx tracking page.
